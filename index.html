Last Ripple | Zayed Sustainability Prize 2021 Video Pitch | Pramodh Chandrashekar

Video Pitch Submission- Zayed Sustainability Prize 2021 . My grandmothers death was the trigger point that initiated my entrepreneurial journey. I was then just 18, stuck in my law school trying find ways to dissociate the taboos surrounding death. Last Ripple was born to connect our emotions with nature! I thought nature was the only thing that was capable of making this happen. Drawing inspiration from a green initiative from Italy, we founded Last Ripple to introduce Green funerals in India and to bring in positivity and a sense of belongingness to nature and our emotions, to make Memory Trees! This prize would help us convert our dreams into a reality of creating a 'Green memory forest' in every city instead of graveyards filled with tombstones and sorrow. We also embarked on creating a Mental Health Support redressal forum in every metro in India to give the bereaved mental assistance! Assisting the ecological and mental well-being of the public on this regard! Lets rethink life! Please help us re-imagine life to make this happen!!!
